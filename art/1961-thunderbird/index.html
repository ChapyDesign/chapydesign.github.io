1961 Ford Thunderbird

Hand-Sandcarved and Painted Wood Relief

This original work presents the iconic 1961 Ford Thunderbird as a sculptural wood relief, blending mid-century automotive design with a distinctive handcrafted technique.

Employing a proprietary hand-guided sandcarving process developed by the artist, the composition is deeply engraved into premium hardwood to a varying depth of 1/8" to 3/16". The resulting three-dimensional surface faithfully renders the Thunderbird’s sweeping profile, distinctive round taillights, and elegant chrome detailing in crisp, flowing lines that catch and reflect light.

The recessed portions of the carving are meticulously hand-painted with vibrant yellow automotive enamel, creating striking contrast against the warm, exposed wood grain. This selective coloration enhances the sense of volume and movement, lending the piece a dynamic, almost luminous presence.

A unique fusion of illustration, relief carving, and painting, “1961 Thunderbird” is a one-of-a-kind artwork that celebrates American design heritage through contemporary wood artistry. Signed by the artist and ready to hang.
